MDM - Project 2

The Age Classifier model is an advanced deep learning model designed to predict the age group of individuals based on their facial features. Developed using state-of-the-art techniques in computer vision and machine learning, this model leverages the power of convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and the Vision Transformer (ViT) architecture. Trained on large-scale datasets containing diverse images of faces, the model exhibits high accuracy and robustness in estimating age groups ranging from children to senior adults.
